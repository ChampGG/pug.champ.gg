About PugChamp

the world's best PUG service

The Basics

PugChamp was conceived as a successor to tf2pug.me, a similar web-based draft-organized PUG system. It aimed to not only recreate the same functionality but add to it with features like ratings and automated servers.

After a rapid month of development, the service went into open alpha in late January 2016. Rapid testing and refinements let the system move into beta in mid-February, at which time special Pro League services were established alongside the open systems.

Open Source

The base PugChamp system is fully open source - help contribute to it or use it in your own project!
